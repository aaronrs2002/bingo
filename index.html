Bingo rules:

Players can win by covering a complete horizontal, vertical or diagonal line. Pattern bingo is often played in 75-ball games. A pattern or shape will be declared at the start of the game and players are aiming to cover their numbers to make this pattern on the card.

To start select the amount you are willing to bet from the top. Select "Next Square" to start. While the other players automatically fill in the square their card has, you need to locate the square called on your card. If you do not have this square, select "next square" to move to the next one.
